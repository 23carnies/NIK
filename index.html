Not in Kansas, Quilts by Dorothy

Hello and welcome to my quilt shop. I'm Dorothy and we are not in Kansas anymore. I've been making quilts for my family and friends for years. I'd love to make one for yours too! Quilts make great show pieces for your bed, couch or even wall hangings. I make them in many sizes from baby blanket to California King and everything in between.

A quilt is a fantastic gift. One that will be passed down for generations. My quilts are put together by hand and quilted by machine. They are sturdy and beautiful.
There are many designs and colors. Check out our gallery of quilts and find one you like. I can adjust the colors to fit your room.

Have a stack of t-shirts from sports or group events? Have them made into a commemorative T-shirt quilt. These keepsakes allow you to display your treasured shirts and keep you warm at night.
